About A Jokeful Day.

A Jokeful Day hosts the largest selection of one-liner and traditional jokes. These jokes have been selectively chosen to provide you with the corniest laughs possible.

This webpage is written and maintained by Jessica Schlesiger.

This webpage utilizes Github Pages and Jekyll to host and generate the pages. It is completely open source, so please feel free to view the project over at Github.
